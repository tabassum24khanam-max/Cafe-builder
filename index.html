Our Story

A place where moments are made

ON Snack Bar (أون) is Riyadh's destination for those who appreciate the finer details — from a perfectly pulled espresso to a slice of our signature London Cheesecake.

We believe great food is more than sustenance. It's a ritual, a pause in the day, a reason to gather. Every item on our menu is crafted with intention, using quality ingredients that speak for themselves.
