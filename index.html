Our Story

A place where every moment matters

ON (أون) is Riyadh's beloved cafe and brunch destination — a warm, welcoming space where specialty coffee meets artisan desserts and freshly crafted sandwiches. Founded in 2020, we've been creating moments worth savouring.

Every item on our menu is made with care, using quality ingredients. Whether it's your morning cortado, a Cinnabon fresh from the oven, or a London Cheesecake to share — ON is where you want to be.
